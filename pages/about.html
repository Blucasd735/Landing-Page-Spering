Brief history about Spering.


When the cat was sleeping, I thought about the structure of its comfortable sleep.


In the heart of a bustling city, nestled between towering skyscrapers, lies a unique establishment known as "Paws & Profits." This innovative company, unlike any other, is not run by humans but by a team of remarkably talented felines. Led by the charismatic tabby, Chairman Whiskers, the company has carved out a niche for itself in the competitive business world.


At Paws & Profits, the cats handle every aspect of the operation with remarkable efficiency. From customer service to product development, these feline entrepreneurs excel in their roles. They communicate effortlessly using a sophisticated system of meows, purrs, and tail flicks, ensuring seamless coordination and teamwork.


One of the company's most beloved employees is Miss Fluffy, the head of marketing. With her keen eye for aesthetics and unmatched charm, she spearheads creative campaigns that captivate audiences worldwide. Whether it's promoting the latest catnip-infused toys or organizing adoption drives for shelter cats, Miss Fluffy's innovative strategies always leave a pawprint on the industry.
